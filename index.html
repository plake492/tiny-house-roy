About

This is a self built tiny house. it is on a 26 foot tiny home trailer manufactured by American Made. There is a total of 280sqft including two lofts, one bed loft and one multi-purpose loft which can be used as storage, a reading nook or another small bed room. There are plenty of windows to give the house an open feel with plenty of natural light and a spacious atmosphere. the living space includes a dining/work table, and a lovely sitting area, perfect for relaxing, entertaining, or your morning cup of coffee. The bathroom is 5x7 feet with a traditional toilet, sink, shower, cabinet, dresser and clothing rack, complete with a farmhouse door from the 1940's. The kitchen has plenty of room for storage with two cabinets, a drawer cabinet, and a custom pull out drawer pantry. Up the functional storage staircase you'll find the bedroom loft which is cozy but spacious at 9x7ft with two windows, custom side tables, lights and a decorative shelf. Across the house you'll find the second loft, which is 6x7ft. This area is perfect for a small bedroom, a reading nook or simply storage space. Lastly you'll notice a handmade one of a kind stained glass window above the staircase.
